Quality Control

In carrying out its activities, Future Solutions Company is dedicated to continuous operations aligned with the highest quality standards. We adopt and implement cutting-edge quality management and monitoring systems, blending the expertise of our local staff with international ISO standards. This collaboration with foreign experts has significantly enhanced the company's efficiency across all technical aspects. Our projects, spanning design and implementation departments, strictly adhere to the International Building Code (IBC), Mechanical (IMC), and Electrical (IEC). We also follow Iraqi codes like the Building Code Requirements for Reinforced Concrete and Iraqi Standards for Construction Materials.
Furthermore, the company prioritizes staff training for compliance with quality management requirements, aiming to boost operational efficiency. Engineers and technicians are enrolled in the latest training courses, covering comprehensive quality management, PMP project management, occupational safety, environmental suitability, and other relevant technical topics.
